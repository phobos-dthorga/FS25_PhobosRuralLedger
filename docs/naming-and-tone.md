# Naming And Tone

## Chosen Name

**Phobos' Rural Ledger**

The name is serious, grounded, and economic. It suggests that the mod quietly
keeps account of land, debt, yields, obligations, and rural fate without
sounding like a fantasy item or a joke mod.

## Subtitle Options

- Living farm finances for a living countryside.
- Every field has a balance. Every harvest has a cost.

## Tone Rules

- Serious, local, and practical.
- Slightly ominous only where it fits debt, risk, or land pressure.
- Never goofy in player-facing event text.
- Prefer concrete farm consequences over abstract simulation language.
- Let the Phobos identity show through restraint and archival flavor, not
  spectacle.

## Copy Examples

Good:

```text
Credit is tightening across the valley.
Miller Farm requests urgent harvest support after a poor soybean season.
Hay demand is high after two wet weeks delayed baling.
```

Avoid:

```text
Random economic disaster triggered.
NPC balance changed.
Funny money event happened.
```
