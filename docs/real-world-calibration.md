# Real-World Calibration

The real-life basis should be broad rather than overly precise. The design seed
mentions USDA, ABARES, and similar farm-finance categories as useful reference
shapes, not exact balance-sheet targets.

## Useful Category Families

- income;
- expenses;
- debt;
- assets;
- government payments;
- commodity outlook;
- land value;
- risk and disasters;
- farm performance by region, industry, and farm size.

## Calibration Principle

Use real categories to make the system feel plausible, then tune for gameplay.
The player should experience believable pressure and opportunity without needing
to audit a farm business statement.

## Candidate Regional Presets

- US Midwest grain;
- Australian broadacre;
- EU mixed farming;
- Alpine dairy;
- rice region;
- livestock-heavy region;
- high land-value peri-urban region.
